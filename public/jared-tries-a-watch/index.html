Jared Tries a Watch

My collegue-for-another-month-and-a-half Jared Sinclair went to an Apple Store and tried on a few watches.

I have been interested in the link bracelet and I’m not sure if his comments make me more or less interested in one. Although I think that the price of it has already made me more interested in the classic buckle.

Most of all I just want Apple to announce a shipping date for Sweden! Come on guys!!
