The Coordinator Pattern

Soroush Khanlou had a very interesting blog post a while back. In it he elaborates on his “coordinator pattern”. Its a very neat pattern in which you make all of your view controllers “flow agnostic”. Ie. they know nothing about the view controller hierarchy, when to push another view controller on the navigation stack, when to present a modal etc. All of that responsibility is delegated to a coordinator object.
